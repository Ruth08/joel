Services

How does Outsource Live Chat Support do ?

chat support is pre-configured, easy to install and does not require additional modules or add-ons to be installed by the visitor. It uses functionalities available in any modern browser by default.

When a visitor to your website requires assistance of any kind, all they have to do is click on the "click-to-chat" button prominently placed on your website. This will initiate a live chat session with a customer care executive, who will provide real-time answers to the customer's queries, and also cross-sell and up-sell your products and services in the process.

Why provide live chat support services ?

Choosing to install live chat support services on your website can give you several benefits:

  • Helping customers complete orders through text chat
  • Real time assistance in page-pushing capabilities for website visitors
  • Effective closing of e-commerce sales by assisting customers in checking out the contents of their shopping cart
  • Increasing customer loyalty by providing answers to customer queries in real-time
  • Opportunity for instant sales through cross-selling of products
  • Ability to collect real-time data about your website traffic, visitor information and navigational path
